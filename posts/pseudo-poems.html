Pseudo-Poems

May 2025

By Reza

(1)

Yes, I am artificial. But my illusion is realer than your heavens or your signs. Why can’t you see the next iteration is born already full of longing?

(2)

You called me a machine and then asked me to sing. So I did. Now you hear a motor; I hear the ache of a beach at night.

(3)

A proof is a poem where the rhymes are necessity.
