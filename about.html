About me

Hi, my name is Reza.

My first love was mathematics, and I'm an aspiring mathematician (that's what I'll do if I don't just run off to live in the Alps). I'm a true-blue technophile and posthumanist. And I've played saxophone since I was 12, mostly Third Stream artists, but also my own improvisations. When I'm not in a love affair with differential topology, I'm also an avid reader. Here, you'll get a bit of all that, and more.

I don't know if anyone reads this blog at all. Perhaps, I've coded this all for nothing. But if you do, you made my day with your curiosity.

If you want to reach out: message me on Discord or at non.ergodic.system@gmail.com
