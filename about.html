        About me

        Hi, my name is Reza.

        Where should I begin? Oh yes, I love mathematics: long story short, I am a pure math undergrad (and a dropout) and I took a gap year, and now, I’m back in college as a different major. I also deeply love music, physics, literature, philosophy, history, and theology. I am an artist in the sense that I draw, paint, write, and compose music (but only subpar). I do photography. I make experimental videos sometimes. I used to be obsessively into art films, on-and-off. Here, you'll get a bit of all that, and more.

        I'm actually in my early twenties, despite what the page 'About the Author' suggests. I'm a child of the diaspora, the product of an Iranian father and a Malaysian mother. As you'd expect, I speak multiple languages.

        I'm often haunted by a vague sense of destiny and I have a million clownish delusions of grandeur. I also have severe ADHD and complex PTSD; I tend to talk and write a lot about that. During my teenage years up until the pandemic, I’ve had a lot of out-of-body experiences, and occurs occasionally in my dreams, both in conscious daydreams and where I sleepwalk at night (yes, I'm a parasomniac). I'm not sure if I'll ever resolve this but at least I will want to write about it here.

        Some of the most mesmerizing experiences I've had in my life were on mountains in remote areas, far away from the city lights, completely in solitude and surrounded by pine trees in cold weather (and sometimes the gentle sound of rain), where I encountered the brilliance of light that made the trees cast sharper shadows under the moon than in daylight, and the Earth was lit blue finely through the silver river of the Milky Way just above my head).
